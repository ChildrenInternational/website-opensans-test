possible without the help of supporters like you.

You’ll be amazed how much can be accomplished with your monthly contributions.

Your gifts guarantee your sponsored child receives benefits and support that can dramatically change his or her life. We make that possible by maximizing our efficiency and keeping administrative costs low—so that 84 percent of our total expenses support programs for children and youth.

“Never doubt that the seed you are planting is bearing fruit. It will show itself in many different ways. And maybe you will be fortunate enough to share in it. Sometimes, it takes only a couple of words to make a change that lasts a lifetime.”

- Chris K., longtime CI sponsor of 20 kids

see your impact in person

Did you know? One thing that makes CI different is the fact that we encourage our sponsors to visit their sponsored children. On average, we facilitate over 700 sponsor visits every year!

Plan a visit to see your child and tour a community center

HOW TO PLAN A VISIT

Empowering change

CI sponsors are currently helping nearly 220,000 children in 10 countries.

Here are just a few of the things we accomplish together:

  • Operate over 70 community centers in the 10 countries we serve, offering poor children and youth a safe, positive place to learn and grow
  • Provide hundreds of thousands of free medical and dental exams to children in poverty
  • Help thousands of kids overcome obstacles to their education
  • Grant thousands of scholarships each year so qualified youth can further their educations
